Our Mission

Preserving Sindhyat in the Bay Area

To be a premier association of Northern California for promoting and preserving the culture, language, image, and values of the Sindhi Community. Based in California's San Francisco Bay Area, our association thrives on bringing the Sindhi Community together for cultural events, charitable causes, and volunteering opportunities.

"We are grateful to all our mentors, distinguished members and the pillars of our community who are instrumental in supporting all our initiatives."
Focus Areas
