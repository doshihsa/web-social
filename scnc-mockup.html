About SCNC

Keeping "Sindhyat" Alive — one celebration, one gathering, one generation at a time.

Our Story
"To be a premier association of Northern California for promoting and preserving culture, language, image and values of the Sindhi Community."

Based in California's San Francisco Bay Area, our association thrives on bringing the Sindhi Community together for various cultural events, charitable causes, and volunteering opportunities.

Our organization is based on a non-profit model to best serve our community's needs. Our main goal is always to bring the Sindhi Community together by organizing cultural events and providing a platform for volunteering opportunities.

We are grateful to all our mentors, distinguished members, and the pillars of our community who are instrumental in supporting all our initiatives. Most of all, we are sincerely grateful to all members of the Sindhi Community who continue to motivate us.

Focus Areas
🌏
Global Events & Networking
Supporting Sindhi Sammelan and networking with sister organizations worldwide.
🙏
Religious Events
Annual Cheti Chand & Guru Nanak Janam. Monthly Chandu Aarti and Palav, Sadhu Vaswani Satsang with Geeta Paath. Weekly Aarti of Jhulelal Sain at Fremont and Sunnyvale Temple.
🎭
Cultural Celebrations
Annual Diwali Gala, Sindhi Tipno, picnics, and community family events.
🤝
Charitable & Volunteering
Providing platforms for community service, mentorship, and charitable giving.
